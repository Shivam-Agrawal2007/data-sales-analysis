A Python-based Sales Analytics Pipeline that processes retail sales data, performs business analysis, generates reports, and visualizes insights through charts and dashboards.

---
How does this help us:
- Load and clean retail sales dataset
- Handle missing values
- Generate business analytics
- Produce CSV reports automatically
- Generate multiple charts
- Create a Sales Analytics Dashboard
- Generate a summary report
- Log project execution

---

Challenges faced during making it:
- Some dates in the datasets are not in same format, so I have to change my code.
- Products name are too long to fit in vertical bargraph so I have to convert them in horizontal bar graph.

---

The project provides:

- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Total Orders
- Unique Customers
- Unique Products
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Top 10 Products
- Top 10 Customers
- Monthly Sales Trend

---
